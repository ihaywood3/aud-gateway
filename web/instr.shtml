Know-Your-Customer Process

Driver's Licence

Please use an Australian driver's licence. You can scan or photograph it with a smartphone. It needs to be legible and the ID clearly visible.

If you have changed address you may need to upload a photo of the back of the licence with your current address on it.

Self-photograph

Please take a photograph of yourself, holding a piece of paper with your BitShares ID on it.

It needs to be clear enough so I can read the piece of paper, and see that you are the same person as on the licence.

BSB and Account Number

This is required to withdraw money (otherwise I have no idea where to send it), but you can leave it blank if you only want to deposit.

Using the Service

Logging In

This website doesn't have a login and you don't set a password here: once you have completed KYC everything is done through the BitShares wallet, except for one deposit method.

I don't assign user IDs: your existing BitShares ID is your identifier for this service.

Depositing Cash

When your account has been verified, you will be e-mailed with confirmation.

1. Australia Post Money Order

You can buy Australia Post Money Orders online and e-mail the money order to money.orders@river-stone.com.au. Quote your BitShares ID in the e-mail.

These take 1–2 business days to process. Make the money order to “River Stone Technologies.” The money order costs $6.95 and my processing fee is 1.1%.

NOTE: only electronic money orders are accepted, not paper ones.

2. Bank cash deposit

You can deposit cash at a Commonweath Bank branch into the facility account. My processing fee is 1.1% and there is no extra fee. Again processing will take 1–2 business days.
