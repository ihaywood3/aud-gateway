Know-Your-Customer Process

Driver's Licence

Please use an Australian driver's licence. You can scan or photograph it with a smartphone. It needs to be legible and the ID clearly visible.

If you have changed address you may need to upload a photo of the back of the licence with your current address on it.

Self-photograph

Please take a photograph of yourself, holding a piece of paper with your BitShares ID on it.

It needs to be clear enough so I can read the piece of paper, and see that you are the same person as on the licence.

BSB and Account Number

This is required to withdraw money (otherwise I have no idea where to send it), but you can leave it blank if you only want to deposit.

Using the Service

Logging In

This website doesn't have a login and you don't set a password: once you have completed KYC everything is done through the BitShares wallet.

Depositing Cash

When your account has been verified, you will be e-mailed with BPay ID and biller ID. You can make payments using Australia Post or online banking. Once processed (which can take 1--2 business days) the corresponding amount (minus fees) will be deposited in your BitShares account, as RIVER.AUD tokens.

Through the BitShares DEX you can then sell the tokens for BTS (the core BitShares currency), you need to refer to the BitShares documentation for how to do this.

Withdrawing Cash

You need to purchase RIVER.AUD tokens on the BitShares DEX

Then send the tokens to BitShares account river.aud4

The same amount, less fees, will be same to your account by EFT, again 1--2 business days.

Problems

Please contact info@river-stone.com.au. Please include your BitShares ID and the transaction ID if known.
